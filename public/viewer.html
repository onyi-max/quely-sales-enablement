The problem

The full context around a piece of work is spread across too many places.

A ticket has a title and maybe a short description. But the customer call that created the urgency, the discussion about the tradeoff, the meeting where scope changed, and the decision history all live somewhere else. So the work doesn't start when the ticket is assigned. It starts when someone asks around and pieces together what the ticket actually means.
